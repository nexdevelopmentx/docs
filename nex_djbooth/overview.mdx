A DJ booth system for FiveM. Players walk up to a booth, paste a YouTube link, and broadcast it to anyone nearby. You add and manage booths in-game with no config editing.

## What you get

* Works on **Qbox, QBCore, and ESX** — auto-detected
* In-game admin panel — add and remove booths live
* YouTube playback by URL or video ID, no API key needed
* Auto-fetched song titles, thumbnails, and progress bar
* Live stream support with a "Live" badge
* Per-booth sound radius and default volume
* Per-booth job or gang restrictions (or `public` for everyone)
* Optional prop spawning at each booth (e.g. boombox)
* Queue and history tabs — replay any past track with one click
* Pause / resume / stop / volume slider
