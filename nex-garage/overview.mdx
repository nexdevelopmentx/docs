A modern public garage system for FiveM. Players walk up to a garage, open a clean UI, and spawn or store their vehicles. Works on QBCore, Qbox, and ESX out of the box.

## In this section

* [Installation](/nex-garage/installation)
* [Configuration](/nex-garage/configuration)
* [Troubleshooting](/nex-garage/troubleshooting)

## What you get

* Works on **QBCore, Qbox, and ESX** — auto-detected
* 10 pre-configured garages around the map (cars, boats, and aircraft hangars)
* Multiple spawn points per garage so cars don't pile up on each other
* Vehicle type enforcement — cars to car garages, boats to docks, aircraft to hangars
* Player-to-player vehicle transfers
* Garage-to-garage transfers with a configurable fee
* Saves engine, body damage, and fuel between sessions
* Plugs into 8+ vehicle key scripts and 7+ fuel scripts
* Map blips for every garage
* Admin commands to give and remove vehicles
