## What you get

* Works on **ESX, QBCore, and Qbox** — auto-detected
* Dashboard ships ready to use — no build step, nothing to compile
* **Two contract tracks** — quick jobs with a rental truck, and freight jobs that require your own truck
* 60+ cargo loads with **ADR classes** and fragile / valuable / urgent / illegal flags
* **Skill tree** — six skill paths, XP-based leveling up to level 36
* **Truck dealership** — buy, sell, repair, and refuel a fleet, gated by level
* **NPC drivers** — hire drivers, assign them to your trucks, and collect their profits
* **Loans** with automatic daily debits — and bankruptcy if you can't pay
* **Company bank** — a separate trucking wallet that deposits to and withdraws from your framework account
* **Crews** — shared delivery bonuses, passwords, and member limits
* **Police trailer inspection** — officers can check parked trailers for illegal cargo
* Top-earner **leaderboard** built into the dashboard
* `ox_target` support with a marker-and-keypress fallback
* Automatic vehicle key grants for ten popular key resources

## How the job loop works

1. Players walk up to a depot (LSIA freight yard by default) and open the dashboard.
2. The server generates contracts on a timer — by default 5 quick and 5 freight contracts every 5 minutes, capped at 30 active per track.
3. The player picks a contract. Quick jobs spawn a rental truck and trailer at the depot; freight jobs only spawn the trailer, so the player needs an owned truck.
4. They hook up, follow the route blip, and back the trailer into the delivery marker.
5. Payout scales with distance, cargo flags, skill bonuses, and the trailer's condition on arrival. XP is awarded per kilometre, and each level grants a skill point.
