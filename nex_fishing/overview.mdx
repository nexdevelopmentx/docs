A skill-based fishing system for FiveM. Cast anywhere with water, work configurable zones for rarer fish, level up, build renown for permanent perks, and sell your catch — all in a sleek custom UI.

## What you get

* Works on **Qbox, QBCore, and ESX** — auto-detected through a bridge layer
* Fully custom UI — menus, buy/sell dialogs, and an interactive skill check, with full keyboard and mouse navigation
* Configurable accent color (`Config.ui.accent`) and selectable language (`Config.locale`, English and Spanish included)
* Unlimited configurable **fishing zones** plus free open-water fishing anywhere
* 10 fish out of the box, each with its own price range, catch chance, and skill check pattern
* **Level & XP system** with persistent progress (oxmysql) — zones and gear can be level-gated
* **Renown system** — earn reputation per catch and climb tiers for permanent perks: sell bonus, shop discount, boat rental discount
* **Fisherman shop** — buy rods and baits, sell your catch
* **Boat rental** with anchoring and partial refunds on return
* Three rods with different break chances, two baits that shorten the bite wait
* `ox_target`, `qtarget`, and `qb-target` support
* Discord webhook logging for every catch
* 0.00ms idle resmon
* Item definitions and inventory images included in `install/`
