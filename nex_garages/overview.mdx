Personal, job, gang, impound and private garages for cars, boats
and aircraft — with an in-game garage editor so you rarely have to touch a
config file.

## What you get

* Works on **ESX**, **QBCore**, and **Qbox** — auto-detected
* UI ships ready to use — no build step, nothing to compile
* **Public, job, gang, impound and private garages**, each supporting cars, boats or aircraft
* In-game **garage editor** (`/garagesadmin`) — create, edit, hide and teleport to garages live, with JSON import/export between servers
* **Police impound** with selectable lots, reasons, fees, hold timers and an owner-retrievable toggle
* **Auto-impound** for vehicles left out too long
* **Vehicle actions** — transfer between garages or players, share access, sell to players, rename, pin favourites, track left-out vehicles, repair for a fee
* **Persistence** — fuel, engine, body, mods, mileage and per-panel deformation (bundled) survive storage
* **Job spawner garages** — fresh fleet vehicles with livery/extra selection, rank locks and plate prefixes
* Walk-in **garage interiors** ("Go inside") using the import/export showroom
* **Cinematic garage mode (beta)** — optional in-world vehicle browser with a cinematic camera, screen effects and an actions drawer, replacing the list menu
* **Speed-based vehicle classes** (D–S tiers from handling data) to restrict what parks where
* **Economy** — store fees, retrieval costs, capacity limits, society-fund payments
* Vehicle thumbnails from the official FiveM CDN, your own CDN, or per-vehicle links
* In-game audit log panel (`/garagelog`), Discord webhooks (via `server.cfg` convars), nickname word filter, phone exports
* Server-side validation on every money- or ownership-moving action
* English and Spanish locales included
