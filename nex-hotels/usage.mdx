What your players will actually do in-game.

## Renting a room

1. Find a hotel blip on the map.
2. Walk to the front desk and press **E** (or eye-target the receptionist if you're using `ox_target`).
3. Pick a floor, then pick a room. Unavailable rooms are dimmed.
4. Pick how many days you want to rent for.
5. Click **Rent**. The cost comes out of your bank (or cash if bank is empty).
6. A GPS route + cone marker shows you the way to your door.

## The door

Each room door has a floating `[E]` keycap:

* **Red** — locked. Press **E** to unlock.
* **Green** — unlocked. Press **E** to enter (or to lock again from outside).

## Inside the room

Depending on what the admin set up, you might see prompts at:

* **Storage** — your personal stash for this room.
* **Safe** — a separate stash for valuables.
* **Wardrobe** — opens your clothing menu.
* **Minibar** — buy drinks and snacks.

Anything in your storage or safe stays yours — checking out moves it to your recovery locker, not the next renter.

## Extending

Walk to **any** front desk in the same hotel and click **Extend**. Pick more days, pay, done. You can extend as many times as you want.

## Checking out

At any front desk, click **Check Out**. The room is freed instantly, and anything left in storage or the safe goes to your recovery locker.

## Recovery locker

If you forgot to grab your stuff, or your rental expired while you were offline:

1. Walk to any front desk in that hotel.
2. The rent UI shows a **Recovery** section if you have items waiting.
3. Click **Claim** to get them back.

Your recovery locker is per-character and per-hotel, and it survives server restarts.

## Multiple hotels at once

You can hold one room per hotel at the same time. So you can have a room in Opium Nights *and* a room in V Hotel — but not two rooms in the same hotel.
