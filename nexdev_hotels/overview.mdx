A complete hotel system for your FiveM server. Players walk up to a front desk, rent a room for as many days as they want, get a GPS marker to their door, lock and unlock with a single key press, and use storage, a safe, a wardrobe, and a minibar inside the room — all owned by their character and surviving server restarts.

You manage everything through an in-game admin panel (`/hoteladmin`). No editing config files, no restarts.

## What you get

* Works on **Qbox, QBCore, ESX, and Ox Core**
* Day-based rentals players can extend at any front desk
* Pays from bank or cash automatically
* In-room storage, safe, wardrobe, and minibar (each is optional per room)
* Recovery locker for items left behind when a rental ends
* Floating `[E]` keycap on the door — red when locked, green when unlocked
* GPS marker to the room's door after renting
* Built-in admin panel with a door picker — no manual coordinates
* Three drop-in hotel imports for popular interiors (Opium Nights, Richards Cortez, V Hotel)
