Need a hand? Here's how to reach us.

## Discord

The fastest way to get help is our Discord server: [discord.gg/nexdev](https://discord.gg/nexdev).

* **#support** — general questions and setup help
* **#bug-reports** — reproducible issues with logs
* **#feature-requests** — suggestions and feedback

## Website

Browse the full catalog and store at [nexdevelopment.xyz](https://nexdevelopment.xyz).

## Before you open a ticket

Please have ready:

* The script name and version
* Your framework (QBCore, Qbox, ESX, Ox) and its version
* Your `server.cfg` lines for the script and its dependencies
* Server console output around the error
* Steps to reproduce
