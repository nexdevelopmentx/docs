## 2026-06

**Added**

* `nexdev_battlepass` — battle pass with daily/weekly quests, free and premium reward tracks, and a fully in-game rewards editor (`/bpadmin`). Tebex transaction verification or redemption codes for premium.
* `nexdev_businesses` — full business management for restaurants, bars, and shops, with an in-game setup tool, a management dashboard, persisted orders, and society banking.
* `nexdev_polecreator` — admin pole setup system: place, edit, job-lock, and delete poles through a black & white NUI, with a player dance menu.
* `nexdev_scalemenu` — real-time character height and build scaling with a cinematic camera, presets, and a permission stack (ACE, whitelist, Discord roles).

**Removed**

* `nexdev_garage` — discontinued. It is no longer sold or supported, and its documentation has been removed.

## Catalog

Every resource currently documented here:

* `nexdev_battlepass` — quests, free + premium tracks, in-game rewards editor.
* `nexdev_bossmenu` — multi-framework boss menu for society banking, hiring, and job management.
* `nexdev_businesses` — restaurants, bars, and shops with in-game setup and a management dashboard.
* `nexdev_coordstool` — in-game coordinate saver (JSON, Lua table, vector3, vector4).
* `nexdev_crafting` — multi-framework crafting with an in-game creator panel, XP, and access rules.
* `nexdev_crutchsystem` — crutch and injury system for players and EMS.
* `nexdev_dealerships` — player-owned dealership lots with financing, stock, employees, and a showroom UI.
* `nexdev_djbooth` — YouTube-powered DJ booths with positional audio.
* `nexdev_donatorsystem` — donator store with Tebex integration and an in-game admin panel.
* `nexdev_elevators` — in-game elevator system with a live admin panel.
* `nexdev_hotels` — hotel system with rentals, room storage, and recovery.
* `nexdev_itemwhitelist` — lock `ox_inventory` items by player or job, managed in-game.
* `nexdev_loadingscreen` — configurable loading screen with music and server links.
* `nexdev_polecreator` — pole placement with job locks and a dance menu.
* `nexdev_queue` — Discord priority queue with card themes, whitelist, and a live waitlist embed.
* `nexdev_scalemenu` — character height and build scaling with a permission stack.
* `nexdev_shoulderpets` — shoulder pets with configurable models and commands.
* `nexdev_uipack` — drop-in UI reskin for `ox_lib`.
